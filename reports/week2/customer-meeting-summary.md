## Artifacts demonstrated

During the interview with the client, we demonstrated the interactive prototype we'd created in Figma, taking into account the client's visual style and elements requirements discussed earlier during the first call. We also presented the client with some ideas that we decided to incorporate into the graphical interface. The client confirmed that the website's visual style and elements looked roughly as they initially envisioned, but during the meeting, the issue of the rating system was raised. The client changed their mind: they'd originally wanted a star-based rating system, but during the second meeting, when asked for clarification, the client responded that the rating system was being replaced with a simple comment-based system. The client also introduced a new tag for games—"featured." The client also specified that the featured game would be selected weekly by the admin, based on the number of comments.
