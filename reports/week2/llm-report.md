We used AI/LLM tools during Week 2 in a few specific parts of the assignment.

---

## Design and prototyping
To help generate and refine design ideas for the interactive prototype created in Stitch. The tool was used for brainstorming layout options, improving screen structure, and suggesting clearer interface wording. The final prototype was still created and edited by the team.

---

## Interview transcription
For converting our customer interview speech into text and help clean it into a readable transcript. After transcription, we manually reviewed the result, removed sensitive information, and edited it for clarity and accuracy before using it in `customer-meeting-transcript.md` or meeting notes.

---

## User stories editing
To improve the grammar, wording, and structure of the user stories. In particular, it helped us make the stories more consistent with the required format and easier to read. The team reviewed the suggestions and kept only the parts that matched the project scope and the customer’s intent.

---

## Other usage
We also used AI assistance for short writing tasks such as drafting report text, improving section structure, and polishing phrasing in the Week 2 documents. All final decisions, requirements, prioritization, and content selection were made by the team.

--- 

## Verification and responsibility
The team verified the AI-generated output before using it. We did not rely on AI as the source of truth for customer requirements, repository decisions, or final project scope.
